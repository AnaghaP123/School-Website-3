Life at School

A classroom

This was built in 2005.

The classrooms hold up about 30 students with one teacher. They often feature decorations howing what subject is taught there. Math teachers decorate their classrooms with number lines and other math themed decorations, and history classrooms feature history and so on.



The track

This was built in 2005.

The track is used for run days where the P.E. students may run a mile or a two mile or on days with free time, simply walk it while chatting with their friends.



The rotunda

This was built in 2005.

The rotunda is a central point. Students can access the office and the corridors to the health office, ASB, library, and other buildings from here. They also meet here after school while waiting to be picked up.

Achievements

Inter-School Debate on Enviorment Pollution
Won first place in the inter-school debate on enviormental pollution

All of the middle school districts competed in this debate. In each round 2 students from different schools would compete. In the finals, both of the students were from this school. We won this inter-school debate and recieved a trophy.

District Soccer Competition
Came in second place in the competition

All of the middle school districts competed in this soccer competition. Ecah school had a team of 15 people. The teams would go against each other. In the final match, it was between us and another school. We lost the finals but came in second place.

Inter-School Science Fair
Came in second place in the inter-school science fair.

The school picked the 12 best science students and they worked together to create a project. Each of the judges rated on a scle of 1-10, and the total was out of 30. Our score was a 29/30 and the 1st placing project got a 30/30.
